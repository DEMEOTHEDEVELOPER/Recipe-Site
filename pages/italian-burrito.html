Italian Burrito

Description:

This is a delicacy recently discovered by my little brother. This is where Mexico, the US and Italy meet in a beautiful combination of deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • Nutella
  • Rice Krispies cereal

Directions

  1. Spread Nutella on tortilla so that it reaches about a half inch from the edge.
  2. Sprinkle Rice Krispies cereal to cover the nutella
  3. Tightly roll the tortilla
