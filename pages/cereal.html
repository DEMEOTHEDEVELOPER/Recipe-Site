Cereal!

Description:

Cereal is a great way to start and finish your day.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of choice
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Pour your cereal of choice into a bowl.
  2. Sprinkle your desired amount of cinnamon into the cereal and mix it a bit
  3. Add as much milk as you want
